Partner Deliver Order Button

The "Partner Deliver Order Button" app streamlines the management of deliveries related to individual customers by consolidating all relevant delivery information in one convenient location. This app allows admins to access delivery details directly through a dedicated button within the customer profile in the backend. The app provides a more efficient and organized approach to managing fulfillment operations by enhancing visibility into customer-related deliveries.

Partner Deliver Order Button Features

Customer Profile Integration:

  • Provides easy access to delivery information via a dedicated button within the customer's backend profile, improving workflow efficiency.

Centralized Delivery Information:

  • Consolidates all deliveries associated with a specific customer, making it easy for admins to access pertinent delivery details in one view.

Time-Saving Filter Functionality:

  • Optimizes the management process by offering a direct link to related deliveries, allowing for quick navigation and handling of orders..

Enhanced Admin Control:

  • It gives admins a streamlined overview of customer deliveries, enabling better organization and tracking of fulfillment operations.

User-Friendly Interface:

  • Designed to be intuitive, allowing admins to navigate delivery information effortlessly without needing extensive training.

Quick Access to Delivery History:

  • Admins can quickly view the history of deliveries associated with each customer.

POS Multi-Currency Payment Workflow

  • Navigate to POS > Configure > Settings and enable or disable multi-currency support for related features.
  • Enable the multi-currency option and input your selected currencies in the POS configuration.
  • Select a product in the POS.
  • With the multi-currency option selected, a popup will allow you to choose a currency, showing the current rate and amount due. Enter the amount, click 'Add,' and close. Unselect the multi-currency option, choose a payment method, and add the remaining amount to the list if needed.
  • Update the amount reflected on the payment receipt.
  • Go to POS orders and select the Payment tab to see payment details in the backend.
